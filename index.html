Our Official Review

Rating: ★★★★☆ (4/5 Stars)

The sequel perfectly captures the sharp wit of the original while updating it for today's social media age. The fashion is immaculate, the dialogue is biting, and Emily Blunt completely steals every scene she is in. It's a must-watch for anyone who loves high-fashion drama.
